Human-AI Interaction, Agentic AI, Applied ML, Video Understanding, Open-World Detection
I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Texas A&M University. My research focuses on Human-AI Interaction and Multimodal AI, developing context-aware and socially-informed AI systems. I work on algorithms that leverage multimodal and egocentric data to understand human behavior and create AI that adapts to diverse user needs and accessibility challenges.
