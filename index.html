Nahyun Kwon

Contact | CV | Google Scholar | Github | LinkedIn

Ph.D. student, Computer Science, HCIED (HCI Engineering & Design) Lab @ Texas A&M University.

My research interest is making technologies more interactive for people. I would like to integrate human-computer interaction (HCI) to data-driven approaches using various devices such as mobile devices. I am interested in implementing computer vision technologies, data visualization and machine learning as effective means for developing an interactive system and analyzing data.
